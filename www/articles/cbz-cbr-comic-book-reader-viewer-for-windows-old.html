Home » Articles » List of CBZ, CBR comic book readers and viewers for Windows

Sumatra PDF

Sumatra PDF supports viewing CBZ and CBR files since version 1.5. Sumatra is free, open-source and supports many other formats, like PDF, ePub, MOBI, XPS, DjVu, CHM.

CDisplayEx

CDisplayEx is a Windows-only reader.

ComicRack

ComicRack is a comic book reader and manager. Also has a version for Android.

Comical

Comical is a cross-platform reader for Windows, Mac OS and Linux.

HoneyView

HoneyView is an image viewer with support for CBZ and CBR files.
