Home » Articles » Sources of free eBooks in ePub and mobi formats

Sumatra PDF supports reading eBooks in ePub and Mobi (Kindle) format. Here's a list of website that offer eBooks in those formats for a free download.

Feedbooks

Feedbooks offers both paid and free ebooks.

Internet Archive

Internet Archive has lots of free texts available in ePub and mobi (Kindle) formats.

Project Gutenberg

Project Gutenberg has thousands of free, public domain books that are available for download in ePub and mobi (Kindle) format.

Baen

Baen is a publisher of science fiction books which offers some of their books as a free download in ePub and mobi (Kindle) format.

Smashwords

Smashwords is an eBook publishers and some of their books are free. They are offered for download in ePub and mobi (Kindle) formats.

Munseys

Munseys has over 30 thousand eBooks for download.
